ACCARDI

Accardi is a collaborative team perfume website project developed by me and my friend. Using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, we aimed to create a basic yet visually appealing website with a minimalistic design.Our focus was on showcasing our diverse range of perfumes through a clean and user friendly interface.

While it currently offers essential features, we have plans to expand and enhance the website with additional functionalities in the future. We are excited to continue working together to provide an exceptional online perfume shopping experience while maintaining the elegance and simplicity that define our minimalistic approach.
